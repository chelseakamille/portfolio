An unsolicited (and very brief) redesign of one of my favorite skincare brands.

Low Fidelity Wireframe

For the layout, I decided to go for something where the user can easily see all the important information right from the start. With large headings for the name of the product and its price, it lessens the time for the user to find these information.

Font Family and Color Palette

To make the product stand out, I decided to pick a light gray background and mostly use white and black for the rest of the content.

High Fidelity Wireframe

I stuck with the ideas I had in mind during the early stages and create a simple yet elegant feel for the layout.

Development

It was fun doing this challenge even though it’s quite difficult to redesign something that’s already well established. Nevertheless, it was an enjoyable practice.

For a closer look at the code, please check out my GitHub page dedicated to this project.
